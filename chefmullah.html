RECIPE FOR PANCAKES RECIPE FOR PANCAKES

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup buttermilk
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • cooking spray or extra butter for greasing the pan

    • Procedure:

  • In large a mixing bowl , whisk together the flour, sugar , baking powder , baking soda and salt.
  • In a separate bowl , whisk together the buttermilk, milk,egg and melted butter.
  • pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredientsand gently stir until just combined. Do not overmix; a few lumps are okay.
  • Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.If needed , lightly grease the surface with cooking spray or butter
  • Using a 1/4 cup measuring cup or a ladle , pour the batter onto the skillet , spacing , them apart to avoid sticking together.Cook the pancakes until bubbles form on the surface,about 2-3 minutes
  • Flip the pancakes with a spatula and cook for another 1-2 minutes, or until the golden brown and cooked through.
  • Remove the pancakes from the skillet and repeat with the remaining batter.
  • Serve the pancakes warm with your toppings , such as maple syrup. Fresh fruit or whipped cream.
